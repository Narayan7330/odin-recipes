Iced Caramel Macchiato

Description

A popular cold coffee drink that layers coffee, milk, and caramel for a sweet and refreshing treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso (or 1/2 cup strong brewed coffee)
  • 1 cup milk (whole, almond, or oat milk)
  • 1 tbsp caramel sauce
  • 1 tsp vanilla syrup (optional)
  • Ice cubes
  • Extra caramel for drizzle

Steps

  1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  2. Pour in the milk and stir in the vanilla syrup if you want extra flavor.
  3. Brew your espresso or strong coffee and let it cool slightly.
  4. Slowly pour the espresso over the milk to create a layered effect.
  5. Drizzle caramel sauce on top.
  6. Stir and enjoy your cool, sweet iced caramel macchiato!
