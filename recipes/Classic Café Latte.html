Classic Café Latte

Description

A creamy, smooth café latte is a go-to for coffee lovers who enjoy the blend of milk and espresso.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso (or strong brewed coffee)
  • 1 cup milk (whole milk or any alternative)
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • Cocoa powder (optional for garnish)

Steps

  1. Brew 1 shot of espresso or strong coffee.
  2. Heat the milk on the stove or microwave until warm. If you have a milk frother, froth the milk until it becomes light and creamy.
  3. Pour the espresso into a cup.
  4. Slowly add the frothed milk to the coffee, holding back the foam with a spoon. Then, spoon the foam on top.
  5. Sprinkle cocoa powder or cinnamon for garnish (optional).
  6. Stir gently and enjoy your café latte!
