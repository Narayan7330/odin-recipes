Mocha Coffee

Description

A rich combination of chocolate and coffee for those who like their coffee with a sweet, indulgent twist.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso (or strong brewed coffee)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp chocolate syrup or melted dark chocolate
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Chocolate shavings or cocoa powder (optional)

Steps

  1. Brew your espresso or strong coffee.
  2. Heat the milk in a saucepan until warm. If you want it frothy, use a milk frother or whisk it vigorously.
  3. Add the chocolate syrup or melted chocolate to the coffee and stir until well blended.
  4. Pour the warm milk into the coffee-chocolate mixture.
  5. Top with whipped cream and sprinkle chocolate shavings or cocoa powder on top.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your decadent mocha coffee!
